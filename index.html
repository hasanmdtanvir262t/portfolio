Biometric Mechatronic Hand

The aim of this project is to design and build a bionic hand that mimics real human motion more closely than many of the current bionic hands in development. Most prosthetic limb designs are restricted by the limitations of myoelectric technology (using electrodes to detect nerve signals and control the mechanism). This project was developed alongside a wearable glove that detects fine motion in the wearer’s hand and acts as a controller. In this way, the bionic hand can quickly and accurately imitate the motion of the user’s hand.
